Ruben C. Salazar Elementary School

Home Of The Golden Eagles

1810 Dement Street, San Bernardino, CA 92407

Phone (909)555-0666 Fax (909)555-0667

Ruben C. Salazar Elementary School opened in 2019 to serve the portion of San Bernardino City Unified School District in, around and just south of Cajon Pass and east of Interstates 15 and 215. It had an enrollment of 645 at the beginning of the 2021-22 school year.

Ruben Salazar (1928-1970) was a Mexican-born American journalist known for his pioneering work covering the Chicano community as a member of the mainstream media. He was killed while covering the National Chicano Moratorium March in Los Angeles on August 29, 1970, which was organized to protest the Vietnam War, in which some believed that a disproportionate number of Latinos served and were killed. At the time, Salazar was news director of KMEX-TV in Los Angeles. He was sitting quietly in a bar by a Los Angeles County sheriff's deputy who fired a tear gas canister into the bar, hitting Salazar in the head and killing him immediately.

NEWS: Anaal Nathrakh detail eleventh album, ‘Endarkenment’!

Birmingham extreme metal duo Anaal Nathrakh just shared the details of ‘Endarkenment’, which will be the band’s eleventh album and follow-up to 2018’s ‘A New Kind Of Horror’.

Produced by Mick Kenney (one half of Anaal Nathrakh), ‘Endarkenment’ will be released on October 2nd 2020 via Metal Blade Records.
